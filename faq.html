Frequently asked questions

What is your refund/cancellation policy?
Unfortunately, since we provide proprietary knowledge, signals, and tools, we cannot offer you a refund. However, if you wish to cancel your subscription, you are free to do so.
How can I manage/cancel my membership?
Click the Sign In link to access your account. From there click on your profile and select My Profile. To cancel your reccurring subscription, just select the subscription and then click "cancel subscription".
How can I get in contact with you?
Navigate to Contact Us and submit your request/question and someone will respond shortly. Thank you for your patience!
How long have you been trading for?
Our team of analysts have been trading options for over 12 years total. They each have tons of experience in all market conditions and are always there to help you be the best trader you can be.
Do you personally profit if people buy these options contracts that are suggested?
Absolutely NOT. The companies will report their earnings with or without our intervention and their stock will go up or down in reaction to it. All we are doing is the leg work on the front end to research and speculate, so that we can make our own picks prior to these companies announcing. We then make a post so everyone can see them and trade along once they do their own due diligence.
What is your success rate?
Every single trade that has ever been signaled will be in the #daily-recap channel at the very top of the Discord as well as on the official Future Elites Instagram. After 1 year in business our success rate is roughly 90%.
How much money should I start with?
Obviously, it's always good to have more money starting out. However, we recommend having a minimum of $1,000 available to trade with so that you can properly diversify your portfolio.
Any advice for when to sell?
We usually recommend Trimming most profits between 15-20%, setting your stoploss to break even, and slowly scaling out. For stoplosses we recommend using a 20% stoploss for daytrades and a 50% stoploss for swing trades.
